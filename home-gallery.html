About

Hello, we’re Joe and Teresa Bianco and we’ve been doing property photography in the Puget Sound area for over 15 years. We love photographing homes, apartments, new construction, and remodel work. Teresa especially enjoys doing virtual staging for any empty property you are highlighting. We encourage you to look through our photo galleries and pricing page. We look forward to any questions you may have and also hope to have the privilege of working with you someday soon.
